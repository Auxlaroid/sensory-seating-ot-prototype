⚠️ RESEARCH PROTOTYPE – NOT FOR CLINICAL USE
This interactive tool is part of a research study on sensory‑informed seating allocation. It is not a certified medical device and should not be used for real passenger screening or in‑flight emergencies without independent validation and regulatory approval. All data remains local to your browser; no information is collected or transmitted. The decibel values are illustrative, not real‑time sensor data. The algorithm is rule‑based and not clinically validated.
🔊 Auditory decibel mapping (75–95 dB): Front cabin (75‑78dB) – lowest noise | Mid cabin (82‑85dB) | Rear/engine (88‑92dB) | Galley areas (95dB spikes).
Chronic noise exposure increases cortisol, heart rate, and may trigger sensory overload or arrhythmic events.
